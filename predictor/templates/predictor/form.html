About This Project

This website predicts possible diseases based on your selected symptoms using a rule-based offline engine. No personal data is stored. For more information on diseases, visit the CDC Diseases & Conditions page.

Created for educational and demonstration purposes. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?
Yes, your symptom selections are never stored or shared. All predictions are made instantly and locally.
How accurate is this tool?
This tool uses a rule-based engine and is for educational/demo use only. For real medical advice, always consult a doctor.
Where can I learn more about symptoms and diseases?
Check out the WebMD Symptoms Guide or the CDC Diseases & Conditions page.
